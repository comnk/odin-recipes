Carbonara

Description

One of the most iconic Roman pasta dishes

Carbonara is a simple pasta dish that is rich and creamy and full of flavor

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Guanciale
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water and add salt to water
  2. Add pasta to boiling water and boil until al dente
  3. Dice guanciale into medium size pieces
  4. Grate parmesan cheese into a bowl
  5. Crack eggs into same bowl and mix cheese and eggs with pasta water
  6. Add guanciale to pan over medium heat and cook until crisp around edges
  7. Drain pasta and add pasta to pan
  8. Add cheese egg mixture to pan and stir constantly and intensely
  9. Turn off heat add cracked black pepper, serve immediately
