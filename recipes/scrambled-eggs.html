Scrambled Eggs

Description

The best scrambled eggs are light, fluffy, and taste amazing

One of the more simple recipes you can cook. It does not require many ingredients to make them

Ingredients

  • Large eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter
  • Chives (optional)

Steps

  1. Crack and mix salt and eggs in a small bowl
  2. Heat butter in pan over low to medium low heat
  3. Add eggs to pan and stir low and slow constantly to avoid sticking
  4. After eggs are mostly solidified (1-2 minutes cooking) remove from pan
  5. Add black pepper and chives and serve immediately
