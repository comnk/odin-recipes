Fried Rice

Description

"Yangzhou fried rice, also sometimes called Young chow fried rice, is a dish is beloved worldwide for its colorful and balanced mixture of delicious ingredients."

"Perfect for that moment when you need to use up leftover ingredients."

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Day old rice
  • Scallions / Green onions
  • Carrots
  • Char siu
  • Deveined shrimp
  • Peas
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil

Steps

  1. Cut char siu, scallions, and carrots into medium sized pieces
  2. Boil peas and carrots for 30-40 seconds
  3. Cut shrimp in half and cook shrimp in boiling water for 3 minutes
  4. Crack and mix eggs with salt in a small bowl
  5. Heat wok over medium heat and add rice and eggs to wok
  6. Add char siu, white parts of scallions, shrimp, peas, carrots to wok
  7. Add soy sauce and sesame oil into wok
  8. Mix all ingredients together in wok and serve immediately
